四眼书虫悠琪是谁？
小说漫改《威尼斯十日-马可波罗的密藏》- 1
(图片由本人使用AIGC创作)

Modern Line Female Lead - Chu Youqi/Uki: 24 years old, a Ph.D. in historical archaeology specializing in the history of the Song, Liao, Jin, and Yuan dynasties at Shanghai Danxi University.
Possesses photographic memory, yet deliberately conceals her talent, determined not to be perceived as a genius.
A “four-eyed bookworm” with an innocent and sweet appearance, kind-hearted and easy-going on the inside, yet proficient in social intricacies and decisive in her actions.
Her joking signature: Mastering both Chinese and Western studies... merely scratching the skin, knowledgeable in literature, history, and philosophy... barely touching the surface.
Personal motto: Liberty and love, these two I must have. For my love, I scarifice my life. For liberty I'll sacrifice, my love. - Sándor Petőfi

现代线女主-楚悠琪/Uki：
24岁，上海旦夕大学历史考古学博士，主修宋辽金元史。
拥有过目不忘的超凡记忆力，却刻意伪装深藏不露，立志不要当天才。
四眼书虫、外表天真甜美、内心善良散漫，但又精通人情世故、行事果断坚决。
玩笑签名：学贯中西...之皮毛，文通史哲...之浅表。
座右铭：生命纯可贵，爱情价更高。若为自由故，两者皆可抛。
